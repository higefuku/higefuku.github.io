ScreenshotPrinter

A Windows app to print screenshots with a single shortcut

About the App

ScreenshotPrinter allows you to capture your screen and automatically print it with just a simple Ctrl + PrintScreen shortcut.

How to Use

  • Press Ctrl + PrintScreen
  • The screen will be captured and automatically sent to the default printer
  • A PNG file will also be saved in your Screenshots folder under Pictures

Privacy Policy

  • ScreenshotPrinter and developer do not collect any personal information or privacy-related information about user.
  • ScreenshotPrinter and developer do not collect information of keyboard typing by user.
  • ScreenshotPrinter and developer do not collect the information of files opened by ScreenshotPrinter.
